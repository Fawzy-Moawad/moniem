ABOUT

I'm a passionate Bakery and Pastry Chef currently residing in Vancouver, BC .
I specialize in preparing a baked goods inspired by global culinary traditions.
My dedication to the art of baking has taken me to various corners of the world.
Each experience has enriched my skills in food safety, HACCP and machine safety.
My educational background includes an AEC/DEP in Skilled Trade Certificate, complemented by continuous learning in areas like HACCP and Essential Food Safety Training.
Away from the kitchen, I am known for my excellent interpersonal and organizational skills.
